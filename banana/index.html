Why do Gemini images have watermark?

Gemini adds visible watermarks to AI-generated images to indicate their origin. This helps identify content created by AI models and prevents misuse or confusion with human-made images.

However, for many creators and designers, these watermarks can be distracting — especially when using Gemini images for presentations, mockups, or internal drafts.

How our Gemini watermark remover works

Our Gemini watermark remover is designed specifically for AI-generated images. Instead of applying generic blur or crop techniques, it analyzes the watermark pattern and removes it while preserving the original image details.

The entire process runs directly in your browser. Your image is never uploaded to a server, ensuring privacy and fast processing. In most cases, the watermark can be removed cleanly without noticeable quality loss.

Does this work for Nano Banana watermark?

Yes. This tool also supports newer Gemini image outputs, including Nano Banana watermark patterns.

As Gemini models evolve, watermark styles may change. We continuously adapt the removal process to support the latest formats.
